Things to Know

  • The resort is fairly large, expect a 5-10 minute walk from your room to the main building/pool
  • There is a coffee shop upstairs of the main building, we are usually there every morning between 7:30-8:00 - best coffee you will ever have
  • Get to the pool early in the morning and leave your towels there to claim a spot before they are all taken
  • Some restaurants may require reservations, the buffets do not. You can make reservations through the Grand Palladium app
  • Every day at noon there are free aqua aerobics/aqua spin classes at the main pool. Bill, Lori, Dan, Courtney, and Rachel are regular participants. They have not been able to convince the other family members to join yet
  • Sunset Cove has all of the activities. You can rent paddle boards, kayaks, scuba gear, etc. There is also tubing available for an extra cost
  • Infinity Terrace & Bar (balcony) at night is the perfect spot for late night cards, live music, and karaoke
  • For late night snacks, The Sports Bar is open 24/7 (nachos, pizza, ice cream, onion rings). Room service is also available until 11pm
  • While all-inclusive, tips are appreciated for good service. We usually bring $50-$100 in singles to tip the staff/bartenders
